About the job
About Us

We're on a mission to make it possible for every person, team, and company to be able to tailor their software to solve any problem and take on any challenge. Computers may be our most powerful tools, but most of us can't build or modify the software we use on them every day. At Notion, we want to change this with focus, design, and craft.

We've been working on this together since 2016, and have customers like Pixar, Mitsubishi, Figma, Plaid, Match Group, and thousands more on this journey with us. Today, we're growing fast and excited for new teammates to join us who are the best at what they do. We're passionate about building a company as diverse and creative as the millions of people Notion reaches worldwide.

About The Role

Notion is used all over the world by a variety of types of people for many different things ranging from business to personal use. One thing they all have in common beyond loving Notion is that the privacy, integrity and availability of their experience in Notion essential for them to be successful in their pursuits. The Security Technical Program Manager will support the ongoing effort to protect our users and team through the leadership of the company wide security program by providing structure, rigor and visibility. The STPM will work with leaders to steward program definition, identify needs, design projects and drive those projects to success that supports the overall health of the security program. The STPM will report directly to the CISO and work alongside their staff as both a supporting partner and a leader in the organization.

What You'll Achieve

You will own the collaboration and planning within Security.
You will own project management as a discipline as well as project tracking and performance metrics for those projects.
You will guide major security programs to success with your excellent planning and leadership skills.
You will help protect the security of Notion by ensure the success of cornerstone projects that meaningfully improve security at Notion.
You will work with teams in engineering, operations, and throughout the rest of notion to improve the performance of major Security programs.

Skills You'll Need To Bring

You have demonstrated success as a TPM in security or security related capacity.
You have experience working with engineering and non engineering teams simultaneously.
You have experience influencing change through building buy in and persuasion.
You have strong problem solving skills and a proven track record of making meaningful change through program management.
You have exceptional communication skills that allow you to convey critical details regardless of your audiences technical background.

Nice To Haves

You have a Computer Science background or equivalent technical experience.
You have worked at a fast-growing start-up or SaaS with a high volume user base and are eager to contribute in such an environment (being a current Notion user would be great!).
You have hands-on experience in designing and scaling a Program Management function.
You've heard of computing pioneers like Ada Lovelace, Douglas Engelbart, Alan Kay, and others—and understand why we're big fans of their work.

We hire talented and passionate people from a variety of backgrounds because we want our global employee base to represent the wide diversity of our customers.
